Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: 200g spaghetti, 100g pancetta, 2 eggs, 50g Parmesan, black pepper.

Instructions: Cook pasta. Fry pancetta. Mix eggs and cheese. Combine with hot pasta. Serve with pepper.

Prep time: 15 mins | Serves: 2

Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients: 2 cups mixed veggies (broccoli, bell peppers), 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oil, garlic.

Instructions: Heat oil, add garlic and veggies. Stir-fry for 5-7 mins. Add soy sauce. Serve over rice.

Prep time: 10 mins | Serves: 2
